Symbol Definition: eq

Pragmatic MathML:
<eq/>
Role:
application
Description:

This symbol represents the binary equality function.

description:

This n-ary function is used to indicate that two or more quantities are equal. There must be at least two arguments.

discussion:

The eq element is the relational operator equals. As an n-ary operator, its operands may also be generated as described in Therefore it may take qualifiers.

Property:

Symmetric

Property:

Transitive

Property:

Reflexive

The geq element is the relational operator greater than or equal. The geq element is an n-ary relation. As an n-ary operator, its operands may also be generated as described in Therefore it may take qualifiers.
