Several CDs are under development at RIACA, TU/e, Eindhoven.

The main categories can be found under the headings listed at the top.

For permutation1 we have a phrasebook for GAP as well as a a phrasebook for Magma. These are jar files that can be loaded with ROML, the RIACA OpenMath library.

I have collected some comments on CDs, how to write and how to use them, in the note CD experiences.
